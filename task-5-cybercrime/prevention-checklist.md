# Prevention Checklist: - “Stay Safe Online”
## Do’s
*	Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts 
*	Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible 
*	Always check the website URL before entering personal details 
*	Use trusted and secure networks (avoid public Wi-Fi for transactions) 
*	Keep your apps and devices updated regularly 
*	Verify emails and messages before clicking any links 
*	Use official apps for banking and payments 
## Don’ts
*	Do not share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone 
*	Do not click on suspicious or unknown links 
*	Do not download attachments from unknown sources 
*	Do not trust messages claiming rewards or urgent actions 
*	Do not save sensitive information on shared devices 
*	Do not ignore security warnings from browsers or apps 
## UPI & Financial Safety Tips
*	Never approve unknown payment requests in UPI apps 
*	Always double-check the receiver’s details before sending money 
*	Use UPI apps with app lock or biometric security
*	Double-check payment details before confirming transactions
## Report Cybercrime
*	National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in 
*	Helpline Number: 1930 (24×7)

# Social Media Checklist
## Do's
* Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts.
* Think twice before sharing or commenting on a post.
* Verify facts before sharing news or information.
* Give credit when sharing someone else's content.
* Use professional platforms like LinkedIn for networking. 

## Don'ts
* Don't post sensitive personal info like your home address.
* Don't engage in or encourage cyberbullying. 
* Don't use unprofessional language or offensive slurs.
* Don't post photos of others without their permission. 
* Don't overshare details about your location in real-time.
